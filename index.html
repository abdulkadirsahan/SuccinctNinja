How To Play

Basic Controls:

Swipe or drag your mouse/finger across the screen to slice fruits.

The faster you swipe, the more points you earn!

Scoring System:

• Regular Fruits: 10 points

• Special Glowing Fruits: 25 points + special bonus

• Fast Swipes (Critical Hits): Extra 20 points

• Combos: Chain fruit slices for multipliers (up to 8x)

Lives System:

• You have 3 lives

• Missing fruits costs 1 life

• Slicing bombs costs 1 life

• Special fruits may grant extra lives

Special Items:

• Bombs: AVOID THESE! Slicing costs 1 life

• Glowing Fruits: Give special bonuses like extra points, extra lives, or slowdown effects
